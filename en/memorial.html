Memorial & Archives

Preserving names and historical stories for future generations.

The Virtual Wall

The centerpiece of Global Faith Temple is a half-mile long, 10-foot high marble wall project. It will honor and engrave the names of all Fallen Heroes who died for freedom in the Vietnam War.

Below is the digital archive profile template of a Hero on the Virtual Wall.

Birthplace: Saigon, Vietnam

Year of Birth: 1945

Digital Archives

Besides the physical memorial, we are building a digitized archive. Every name on the wall is not just a text line, but a human being with a family, dreams, and a life story.

The archive will allow researching, searching, and contributing historical materials, helping the younger generation better understand the silent sacrifices.

Gravesite Restoration

We carry out activities to preserve, clean, and care for the graves of the soldiers, ensuring their resting place is always dignified and respected.
